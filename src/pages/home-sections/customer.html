UNISIL CABLE

IGI Cable introduces Unisil Cable. This cable has been built to provide some benefits over Uninyvin Cable.

  • Have a very low smoke density and are halogen free (non poisoning gasses when subject to ignition)
  • The flame VW-1 (the most stringent flame test) when tested in accordance with UL 758.
  • Very Flexible
  • High Ampacity Cables
  • Stable upto 180° C and a high short circuit temperature (and therefore ampacity) of greater than 400° C
